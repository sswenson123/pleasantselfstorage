Frequently Asked Questions

Is the facility gated and secure?

Yes. Pleasant Lake Storage is fully fenced with gated access, so only tenants can enter. Units are clean, dry, and well maintained.

Do you offer outdoor parking for boats, RVs, and trailers?

Yes. We offer 12x40 outdoor parking spaces inside our gated fence for just $40/month — ideal for boats, pontoons, RVs, campers, and trailers.

How do I rent a unit?

Rent 100% online in minutes through our secure rental center — pick your unit, sign, and move in the same day. Or call (218) 227-2899.

How do I pay my rent?

Pay online 24/7 by card or bank transfer (ACH) through the tenant portal. Autopay is available so you never miss a payment.

What unit sizes do you offer?

Indoor units from 8x11 up to 11x28, plus 12x40 outdoor parking spaces. Monthly rates run from $40 to $150 — see the full list above.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes — prepay 12 months and get the 13th month FREE. Ask about first-month-free specials when you rent.

When can I access my unit?

Tenants have access 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, every day of the year.

What areas do you serve?

Hackensack, Walker, Longville, Backus, Pine River, Akeley and the surrounding lake country — minutes from Birch Lake, Ten Mile Lake, and Leech Lake.

What items are prohibited?

No hazardous, flammable, or perishable materials, and nothing illegal. If you're unsure about an item, just ask.

Do you offer protection for stored items?

Yes — tenant protection options are available at move-in. See our Tenant Protection page for details.
