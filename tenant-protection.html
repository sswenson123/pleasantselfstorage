Tenant Protection

Peace of mind for everything you store with us.

Why Tenant Protection Matters

Your homeowner's or renter's insurance may not fully cover belongings kept in a storage unit — and boats, RVs, and seasonal equipment are exactly the kinds of items worth protecting. That's why we make affordable tenant protection available to every tenant at move-in.

How It Works

  • Add it when you rent. Protection options are presented during the online move-in process — choose the coverage level that fits what you're storing.
  • Already have coverage? If your own insurance covers stored property, you can provide proof of that instead.
  • Questions? Call us at (218) 227-2899 and we'll walk you through the options before you commit.
